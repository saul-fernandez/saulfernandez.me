Inspiration

"My biggest inspiration is nonconformity. Without it, everything would not matter to me. Everything would be fine."

I consider myself a privileged person. Thanks to remote work I am able to combine a satisfactory job efficiently with the hobbies that I like most. This has been my biggest transformation, because almost without realizing it, I found myself leading the digital transformation of several companies towards the future of remote work.

I must admit that I was always a student who was never happy with the established education. Disappointed from university education, it took me 3 months decide to leave and go abroad being one of the first Spaniards, before the crisis, to emigrate. In an era where they assured you that if you studied and did not complain, you would have a secure future. Good thing I didn't follow that advice.

I returned with studies and with my professional career on track. Since then, my training has not stopped using any online and self-taught means for it. Coursera, Udacity and recently Udemy are my greatest teachers, not counting the experience gained in the workplace, which I have already expanded on.

I want to give a special thanks to two people.

Marsel Meeusen because he has allowed me to discover remote work, leading the transformation of the Barcelona Graduate School of Economics towards a university of the future. Your trust is for me, priceless.

And to Valentina Thörner , who helped me realize that my experience in remote work has value that I can use as a core of my professional career.
